I'm a service designer with focus on interaction, who believes in solving the problems end to end. That's why my approach is aligned with the Demming cycle(Plan-Do-Check-Act).
When innovating in services one of the most important aspects is to follow a process with a clear set of guiding principles.
I first focus on listening to both business and people, in order to align their needs and create value. Co-creation with every stakeholder is important to put a smile in everyone's face. Measuring and being transparent with information and policies give clarity about the service. Finally, communication and transformation are important to create the desired behaviour of the service and its users.
